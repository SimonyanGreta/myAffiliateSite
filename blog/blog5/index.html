Brewing Methods

Espresso

A concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Pour Over

A slow-drip brewing method using a filter, resulting in a clean and nuanced cup.

French Press

A full-immersion method that steeps coarse coffee grounds in hot water before pressing.

Cold Brew

Coffee brewed with cold water over many hours, yielding a smooth, less acidic flavor.
